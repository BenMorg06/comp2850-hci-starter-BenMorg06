# Job Stories - Week 6 Needs-Finding

## S1:
**Situation**: When my hands are full
**Motivation**: I want to be able to add a task through voice dictation
**Outcome**: So I can add tasks without stopping what I'm doing
**Underlying need**: Because having to stop a task to add one to the app creates frustration and wastes time

**Evidence**: Participant A (notes Q2 & 6) 
**Inclusion risk**: 
**Type**: Job story

---

## S2:
**Situation**: When I have lots of tasks
**Motivation**: I want to be able to prioritise tasks based on urgency
**Outcome**: So I can clearly see which tasks need to be completed first
**Underlying need**: Because having to scroll through a list of tasks and pick out specific ones wastes time and creates cognitive overload

**Evidence**: Participant A (notes Q3), Participant B (Q1), Participant C (Q1), Participant D (Q6)
**Inclusion risk**: 
**Type**: Job Story

---

## S3:
**Situation**: When I have to read a long text
**Motivation**: I want to use the screen-reader to listen to it rather than read it
**Outcome**: So I can make notes whilst hearing the text
**Underlying need**: Having to read a text reduces my ability to make better notes

**Evidence**: Participant C (Q5)
**Inclusion risk**: Screen reader, Cognitive
**Type**: Job Story

---

## S4:
**Situation**: When I have tasks from many different parts of life
**Motivation**: I want to be able to separate tasks by the part of life hey are in
**Outcome**: So I don't get overwhelmed and lose track of tasks
**Underlying need**: Bescause having one big unorganised list creates stress

**Evidence**: Participant D (Q6)
**Inclusion risk**: ADHD, Cognitive
**Type**: Job Story

---

## S5:
**Situation**: When I am working in a room with bad lighting 
**Motivation**: I want to be able to clearly read everything on the screen
**Outcome**: So I don't get strain my eyes and spend more time reading the page than needed
**Underlying need**: Bescause being unable to read the page creates situational disability

**Evidence**: Participant A (Q5), Participant C (Q5)
**Inclusion risk**: Low vision, Colour-blindness, situational
**Type**: Job Story
**WCAG**: 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum, AA) - contrast ratio 4:5:1

---

## S6:
**Situation**: When I am working on my ipad without a mouse
**Motivation**: I need to be able to navigate webpages with just the keyboard
**Outcome**: So that I can surf the web and read the webpage without being excluded
**Underlying need**: Because reliance on pointers excludes some people (limited equiptment, temporary injury etc.)

**Evidence**: Participant D (Q4)
**Inclusion risk**: Temporary Injury, Limited equiptment
**Type**: Job Story
**WCAG**: 2.1.3 Keyboard (No Exception, AAA)
